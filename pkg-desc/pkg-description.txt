OOoLilyPond enables the inclusion of music notation
in OpenOffice.org or LibreOffice Writer, Draw and Impress documents.
The music expression is entered as a text code and then
translated to an image (png, eps or svg) using LilyPond.

http://ooolilypond.sf.net
http://lilypond.org
